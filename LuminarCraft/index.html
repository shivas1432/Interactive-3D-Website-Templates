About LuminarCraft

For over seven decades, LuminarCraft has been at the forefront of lighting innovation. Our commitment to excellence, sustainability, and cutting-edge design has made us the preferred choice for architects, designers, and homeowners worldwide.

From our humble beginnings in 1952 to becoming a global leader in premium lighting solutions, we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in illumination technology.
