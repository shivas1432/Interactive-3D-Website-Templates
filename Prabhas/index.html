Baahubali: The Beginning

2017

The epic conclusion that answered "Why Kattappa killed Baahubali?" This cinematic masterpiece became the highest-grossing Indian film, setting new records and establishing Prabhas as a pan-Indian superstar. A cultural phenomenon that transcended all boundaries.

THE REBEL STAR

From the charming lover boy in "Varsham" to the mighty Baahubali, from the futuristic action hero in "Saaho" to the divine Rama in "Adipurush," Prabhas has constantly reinvented himself. His dedication to cinema, spending five years for the Baahubali saga, and his ability to transcend regional boundaries have made him a true icon of Indian cinema.

Known for his humility, dedication, and larger-than-life screen presence, Prabhas continues to choose scripts that challenge both him as an actor and Indian cinema as an industry. His journey from Tollywood's beloved star to India's pan-national icon represents the evolution of Indian cinema itself.
